# Robot specifications

- 3152g.
## Degrees of freedom
- 6 degrees of freedom and 1 gripper.
## Payload
- 500g.
## Maximum arm span
- Approximately 495-550mm (excluding gripper length).
- Approximately 620-670mm (including gripper length).

- Joint 1: ±90°.
